Banking Information Document - Internal Use

Our Bank provides three major types of financial services:

1. Retail Banking:
   - Savings Accounts
   - Current Accounts
   - Fixed & Recurring Deposits
   - Debit & Credit Cards
   - Home Loans and Personal Loans

2. Corporate Banking:
   - Business Current Accounts
   - Corporate Loans
   - Cash Management Services
   - Trade Finance including Letter of Credit and Bank Guarantees

3. Digital Banking:
   - Mobile Banking App
   - Internet Banking Portal
   - UPI and QR-based payments
   - Chatbot-based Customer Support

Compliance and Security Policies:
- Customer data must remain confidential and secure.
- KYC (Know Your Customer) documents are mandatory before opening any account.
- AML (Anti-Money Laundering) checks are conducted for all high-value transactions.
- Transactions above 10 Lakhs INR require PAN verification.
- Employees must not disclose internal information to any third party.

Customer Service Guidelines:
- Greeting every customer politely and identifying their requirement clearly.
- For card-related issues, ensure immediate blocking if misuse is suspected.
- Digital support should guide users step-by-step through app features.
- Priority customers receive dedicated support channels.

Vision:
To provide secure, fast, and transparent banking services to empower every customer.
